# Software Engineering in the AI Era

<span />

AI coding assistants are now part of everyday development.

This course does not teach you how to use a specific AI tool.

It teaches you how to **supervise, direct, and collaborate** with AI effectively.

---

# The Core Idea

<span />

AI generates code based on what you give it.

The quality of what you get depends on what you provide:

<v-clicks>

- **Context** — project structure, naming, types, documentation
- **Constraints** — tests, linters, CI, type annotations
- **Coordination** — version control, branches, PRs, code review

</v-clicks>

---

# Nothing New Under the Sun

<span />

<div class="h-8" />

The practices in this course were not invented for AI.

They were developed over decades of software engineering to help **human teams** write robust, maintainable, correct code.

<v-clicks>

**What changes**: the same practices now serve a **dual purpose**:
- They help humans collaborate with each other
- **And** they help humans supervise AI

**What doesn't change**: the practices themselves. Context, constraints, and coordination are not new. They are more **valuable** than ever.

</v-clicks>
